Chrome

A surface that reflects everything and remembers nothing. Metallic, minimal, in motion.

Concept

Chrome doesn't have a color. It borrows from everything around it — the sky, the concrete, the light shifting through a window. This design is built around that idea: a surface with no fixed identity, always in reflection, always slightly in motion.

Construction

Dark ground. Animated metallic gradients. Images desaturated until they become texture — hovering reveals what's underneath. The geometry is skewed, angled, never quite square. Blobs drift behind everything like slow weather.
