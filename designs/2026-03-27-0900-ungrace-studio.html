Assessment protocol

Where the orthotic begins

We map the full kinetic chain before a single fitting. The body is not a set of isolated problems — it is a system of compensations, each one a form of imperfect grace.

  • Gait analysis on force plate — 40 metrics captured per step
  • Passive and active range-of-motion assessment at each joint
  • Functional movement screen: the body under load, off-balance, flitting
  • Postural mapping — where your ungrace originates
  • Custom orthotic casting from 3D scan — no standard sizes
